Right understanding

You should be 100% sure that the supplier's understanding is exactly the same as yours, if not you may face unpleasant surprises. There is a global tendency to go for the lowest price that forces the suppliers to cut corners, be it by nibbling on packing material, using inferior raw material, selection of poor quality, and so on… In China, margins are narrow and you will get what you pay for. ISUP can assist in explaining your requirements to the suppliers and get the right price for the right products.

Problems are much easier to solve face to face, with both parties speaking the same tongue.
